About The Artist

Llewelyn Henshaw grew up in South Wales. From a young age he proved to be artistic, winning art prizes in competitions from primary school age onwards. His love for the mountains and ocean pulled him to distant shores across his life. From the steep valleys of the Alps, through the southern sounds of New Zealand, to the tropical beaches of French Polynesia. When not painting you'll find him in the waves. His multi-facetted journey has left him with massive inspiration. Recently, James left his teaching carreer, in search for a more creative and artistic way of living. His journey is currently taking him through Europe on the search for the next inspirational landscape (and perfect waves).
